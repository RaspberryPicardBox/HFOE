Virtually Together: Hull Friends of the Earth

As you might imagine, we have been holding our monthly meetings virtually for much of 2020 and it looks like that might continue for some months yet… Despite a few technical glitches, they have been quite successful with many topics covered: Litter picks, oppositions to planning applications, testing Hull’s new cycle lanes, active transport, tree planting, social media and much more! Even when we go back to in person meetings, it would be great if we could keep the online meetings as an option. It can be an excellent alternative for busy people to connect without the need to travel too far.

We meet at 7pm on the first Thursday of each month. We love to see new faces so if you would like to attend, please contact hello@hfoe.org.uk

We are keen to get more people involved and active in protecting our local area and environment, and welcome every voice.
